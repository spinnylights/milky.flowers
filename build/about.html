I'm Zoë Sparks, an artist working with language, music, and computer programs. Some of my work falls distinctly into one of those media; some of it is more interstitial. i'm glittery milkiness. sheep, LCD puddles, interference patterns, infinite fields of primroses, a universe flooded by the ocean…

I'm trans, was born in 1990, and grew up in Austin, Texas. I graduated from Simon's Rock in 2010 with a B.A. in music theory and composition.

If you'd like to get in touch with me, Twitter and email both work well.
